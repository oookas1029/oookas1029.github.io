Privacy Policy

The "Ki-Log" application (hereinafter referred to as "the App") recognizes the protection of user privacy as a critical responsibility and is committed to the appropriate management and protection of personal information.

2. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

2.1 Information Provided by the User

  • Data entered within the App (settings, notes, physical condition/health logs)

Note: The App does not access your location data or contacts.

3. Purpose of Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

We will not provide collected information to third parties except in the following cases:

The privacy policies of these respective services also apply.

6. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Request to stop the use of or delete personal information

  • Request correction or addition of personal information

For these requests, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The App may use the following technologies to provide better service:

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
